About Skillspark

Empowering Skills. Creating Opportunities.

Skillspark Vocational Academy was founded to address a specific and urgent gap in Lagos: thousands of secondary school graduates who complete their WAEC or NECO exams and then have nothing to do while they wait for university admission. Many wait for years. During that time, they have no income, no skills, and no economic independence.
We believe that gap year does not have to be wasted. In 3 to 6 months, a young person can acquire a marketable skill, learn the basics of running a business, and start earning. That is exactly what Skillspark is built to deliver.
Our vision
To be among the top three youth-focused vocational training centers in Lagos within five years, known for empowering graduates with practical skills and entrepreneurial capacity.
Our mission
To offer affordable, practical, market-aligned vocational training to secondary school graduates in Lagos, equipping them with skills and entrepreneurship knowledge to create their own income.
